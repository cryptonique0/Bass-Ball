## Interactive Elements

### Stat Bars
- Yellow bar extends left to right (Home)
- Cyan bar extends right to left (Away)
- Percentage shown if >20% of bar width
- Winner highlighted with brighter color

### Strength Bars
- Gradient fill shows power level
- Filled portion represents 0-100 scale
- Smooth transitions on update

### Cards
- Hover effects (subtle shadow increase)
- Scroll capability for long content
- Border colors indicate category

### Modal
- Darkened background for focus
- Fixed positioning for stability
- Scrollable content area
- Close button in multiple locations
